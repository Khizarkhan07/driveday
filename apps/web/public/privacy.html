Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how DayDrive ("we", "us", "our") collects and uses your personal data when you visit DayDrive.co.uk or buy a policy. We are the data controller for that personal data.

1. Who we are

We are DayDrive.

Office: 37th Floor, 1 Canada Square, Canary Wharf Estate, London E14 5AA

Email: policy@daydrive.co.uk

Please use this email for any questions about this policy or your data.

2. The data we collect

  • Identity & contact: name, date of birth, email, phone, address.
  • Driving & vehicle: licence number, licence history, penalty points, vehicle registration and details.
  • Policy & claims: cover dates, quotes, claims and declarations.
  • Payment: card details are processed by our payment provider; we do not store full card numbers.
  • Technical: IP address, device and usage data collected via cookies (see our Cookie Policy).

3. How and why we use it

4. Who we share it with

  • Our insurer/underwriter Highway Insurance Company Limited and their agents.
  • Fraud-prevention and insurance industry databases (for example the Claims and Underwriting Exchange, the Motor Insurance Database and recognised fraud-prevention agencies).
  • Service providers (payments, email delivery, hosting, vehicle lookup).
  • Regulators, law enforcement or others where required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Fraud prevention

To prevent fraud and money laundering we, our underwriter and fraud-prevention agencies may check and record your details. If false or inaccurate information is provided and fraud is identified, details may be passed to such agencies and used by other organisations. Further details are available on request.

6. International transfers

Where personal data is processed outside the UK, we use appropriate safeguards such as UK adequacy regulations or the International Data Transfer Agreement.

7. How long we keep it

We keep policy and claims records for as long as needed to administer cover and meet legal, regulatory and insurance requirements (typically up to 7 years after a policy or claim ends). Marketing data is kept until you withdraw consent.

8. Your rights

Under UK data protection law you can request access to your data, correction, erasure, restriction, portability, and you can object to certain processing or withdraw consent. To exercise these, email policy@daydrive.co.uk.

You can also complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk or 0303 123 1113, though we ask you to contact us first.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always published here.
