Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit DayDrive.co.uk. They help the site work and let us understand how it is used. This policy explains what we use and how to control them.

1. Categories of cookies we use

Strictly necessary

Required for the site to function, for example keeping your quote session active and securing forms. These cannot be switched off.

Analytics

Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. Set only with your consent.

Marketing

Used to measure and tailor advertising. Set only with your consent.

3. Managing your choices

When you first visit, our consent banner lets you accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can change your choice at any time via cookie settings. You can also block or delete cookies in your browser settings, though some features may stop working.

4. Changes

We may update this policy as the cookies we use change. The latest version is always published here.
