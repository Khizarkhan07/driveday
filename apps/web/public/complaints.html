Complaints

We want every DayDrive customer to be happy with our service. If something has gone wrong, tell us and we will put it right as quickly as we can.

1. How to complain

Email us with your name, policy or quote reference, what happened and how you would like it resolved:

  • Email: policy@daydrive.co.uk
  • Office: 37th Floor, 1 Canada Square, Canary Wharf Estate, London E14 5AA

2. What happens next

  • We will acknowledge your complaint promptly, normally within 3 business days.
  • We will investigate and aim to resolve it as soon as possible.
  • If we cannot resolve it within 8 weeks, we will write to explain why and tell you when you can expect an answer.

3. If your complaint is about the cover or a claim

Some complaints relate to the underwriting or claims handled by our insurer, Highway Insurance Company Limited. Where that is the case we will pass your complaint to them and let you know, or you can contact them directly.

4. Financial Ombudsman Service

If you are not happy with our final response, or eight weeks have passed since you first complained, you may be able to refer your complaint to the Financial Ombudsman Service. It is free and independent.

  • Website: financial-ombudsman.org.uk
  • Phone: 0800 023 4567
  • Email: complaint.info@financial-ombudsman.org.uk
  • Post: Financial Ombudsman Service, Exchange Tower, London E14 9SR

You normally need to refer your complaint to the Ombudsman within six months of our final response.
